Tomato Soup by Marek

Description

Tomato soup we are gonna make today is my go-to comfort food.

It's easy to make, delicious to taste and soothes your soul.

Now! To the fun part!

Ingridients-Shopping list

  • fresh tomatoes
  • onion
  • garlic
  • celery
  • carrots
  • potato
  • salt, pepper, rosemary

Steps-to-make

  1. Put everything on baking tray.
  2. Sprinkle everything with lots of olive oil. Add freshly grinded pepper and salt.
  3. Put in the oven at 180 degrees until everything is cooked then put on a grill for 2 min.
  4. Put everything in a pot. Blend it - if it gets too thick then add some warm water.
  5. Voila! Serve it with some fresh basil if u like.
