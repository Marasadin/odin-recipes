Grilled Cheese by Marek

Description

Grilled cheese we are gonna make today is my go-to comfort food.

It's easy to make, delicious to taste and soothes your soul.

Now! To the fun part!

Ingridients-Shopping list

  • rye bread
  • cheese of your choice
  • mayo
  • salt, pepper

Steps-to-make

  1. Smear mayo on both sides of rye bread peaces.
  2. Add cheese in between.
  3. Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Sear it on both sides until brown.
  5. Voila! Serve it with some rucola if u like.
